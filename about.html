About Empowered Path Network

Who we are, what we stand for, and why we exist

Our Story

The Empowered Path Network (EPN) was founded on February 26, 2025, by Alpha K. Weah and Omega K. Weah, with a vision to uplift and empower young people.

Witnessing the untapped potential within communities, our founders envisioned an organization that would not only provide resources but also instill a deep sense of purpose and self-belief

From humble beginnings, EPN has grown into a committed network, dedicated to guiding youth through their personal, educational, and professional journeys, helping them discover and cultivate their unique, God-given talents.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower young people by preparing them for a brighter future, helping them recognize and nurture their God-given talents, and fostering a sense of self-worth and motivation. The Empowered Path Network is committed to providing young individuals with the tools, resources, and support needed to thrive and succeed in their personal and professional lives.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every young person is equipped with the confidence, skills, and inspiration to pursue their dreams and contribute positively to society, living in harmony with their God-given purpose. Our vision is to create a generation of empowered youth who see endless possibilities in their future and lead with compassion, integrity, and determination.

Our Core Values

At the heart of Empowered Path Network are the values that guide our every action and interaction:

Empowerment

We believe in the inherent potential of every young individual. We are dedicated to creating opportunities for them to grow, succeed, and make a tangible difference in their communities

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all our endeavors, fostering an environment of trust and mutual respect among youth and communities.

Unity

We promote collaboration and mutual support.

Faith

We hold a profound belief in the power of God-given gifts. We actively encourage youth to embrace their unique abilities and talents as they strive to fulfill their life's purpose.

Inspiration

Our goal is to motivate and uplift young people, helping them discover profound joy, cultivate their passions, and find true purpose in their lives.
