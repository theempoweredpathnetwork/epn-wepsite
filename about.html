About Empowered Path Network

Who we are, what we stand for, and why we exist

Our Story

The Empowered Path Network (EPN) was founded on February 26, 2025, by Alpha K. Weah and Omega K. Weah, with a vision to uplift and empower young people.

Witnessing the untapped potential within communities, our founders envisioned an organization that would not only provide resources but also instill a deep sense of purpose and self-belief.

From humble beginnings, EPN has grown into a committed network, dedicated to guiding youth through their personal, educational, and professional journeys, helping them discover and cultivate their unique talents.

Our Mission

Empower young people by preparing them for a brighter future, helping them recognize their talents, and providing tools and support for success.

Our Vision

A world where every young person is confident, skilled, and inspired to pursue their dreams and contribute positively to society.

Our Core Values

The values that guide everything we do

Empowerment

Inspiration
